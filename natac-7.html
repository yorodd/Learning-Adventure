🎯 Goal Be the first to reach 10 Victory Points. Build outposts and colonies, control trade routes, and play development cards.
🔄 Each Turn
1️⃣Optionally play a Knight dev card
2️⃣Roll the dice — all matching hexes produce resources
3️⃣Build, trade, or buy dev cards
4️⃣End your turn
🏗 Building Costs
🛤Hyperspace Lane⛽+🪨
🏠Outpost (1 VP)⛽+🪨+💨+⚡
🏙Colony (2 VP)💎💎💎+⚡⚡
🃏Dev Card💎+⚡+💨
🌌 Tiles & Resources
🌌 Nebula → ⛽ Fuel
☄️ Asteroids → 🪨 Titanium
🪐 Gas Giant → 💨 Oxygen
⚡ Solar Array → ⚡ Energy
💎 Crystal Moon → 💎 Crystals
🕳️ Void → no resources
⚓ Space Docks (Ports)
🌌Any 3 of same resource → 1 of any (3:1)
⛽🪨💨⚡💎2 of that resource → 1 of any (2:1)
Default bank rate: 4 of same → 1 of any
🏆 Victory Points
🏠Outpost = 1 VP each (max 5)
🏙Colony = 2 VP each (max 4)
🛤Longest Lane (5+) = +2 VP
⚔Largest Fleet (3+ knights) = +2 VP
🃏VP dev cards = +1 VP each (hidden!)
🎲 Rolling 7 — Gravity Storm No resources are produced. Players with 7+ cards must discard half. Then move the robber to any tile — it blocks that tile and you steal a card from a player there.
💡 Tips
•Start near ports that match your tile resources
•6 and 8 produce most often — settle near them early
•Knight cards protect against the robber
•Keep your VP dev cards secret until you win!
